MY HOBBY

My hobby is basically Singing.

I feel relaxed,reduced stress,anxiety and increase feelings of trust,well-being,inner peace and joy whenever i am singing.

I have not really had oppourtunity to travel to another state just to go and sing, however i have been oppourtuned to sing and lead worship sessions in my local church here in Lagos. It is always a sweet and refreshing moment whenever i do so.
Also, i sing whenever i get to do chores, with friends and sometimes compose little lyrics which i love to do more. People who hear me sing always admire my voice when i sing and that gives me encouragement to do more.
